About Us

Josef’s Pastry has been owned and operated by *Josef Hartung since 1984. In *1993, partner Andrea Dadourian joined Josef and they have been working together ever since. Josef has been an expert pastry chef since the early 70’s, where he started learning his art in Suessen and later Stuttgart, Germany. He was also head pastry chef at Berner’s Cafe in Goppinger, Germany from 1980-82, and at Aschbacher Cafe from 1982-84. Upon moving to America, he acquired his current bakery store from Peter & Inger Graf, who opened the store in February of 1975.
