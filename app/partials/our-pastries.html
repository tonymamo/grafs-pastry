Our Pastries

We specialize in a wide variety of European-style baked goods, including cookies, cakes, tortes, pastries, flans, and more. What makes something European-style, you may ask? Good question! When creating baked goods and desserts, the traditional European philosophy is to focus on a delicious, complex flavor, not just making it really sweet (like traditional American desserts). For example, European chocalate tends to be richer in cocoa, as opposed to American chocolate which is usually sweeter.
